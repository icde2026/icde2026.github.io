42nd IEEE International Conference on Data Engineering (ICDE) Montréal, Canada - May 4-8, 2026

Travel

ICDE 2026 will take place in Fairmont The Queen Elizabeth in Montréal, a major commercial hub well-connected to all destinations by air and land.

Fairmont The Queen Elizabeth 900 René Lévesque Blvd West Montréal, QC H3B 4A5, CA View on Google Maps

Arriving by Air

The closest airport is Montréal–Pierre Elliott Trudeau International Airport (YUL). The airport is a major North American gateway served by all major international airlines.

Airport Shuttle Bus (747 Express Bus)

  • This is the most cost-effective public transport option, running 24/7 between the airport and downtown Montréal.
  • Duration: Approximately 35–45 minutes, depending on traffic.
  • Cost: The fare ($11.00 CAD, subject to change) is valid for 24 hours across the entire STM bus and metro network.

Taxi / Ride-Share

  • Option: The quickest and most direct way to reach the hotel.
  • Duration: Approximately 15–20 minutes.
  • Cost: Taxis operate on a fixed rate (approximately $40–$50 CAD, before tip) from YUL to the downtown area. Uber and Lyft are also readily available.

Arriving by Train

The conference venue is uniquely accessible, as it is located directly above Montreal Central Station (Gare Centrale).
  • Accessibility: Guests arriving via VIA Rail Canada (major Canadian routes) or Amtrak (from the US, e.g., New York) can access the hotel lobby immediately from the station.

Getting Around the City (Metro)

The hotel is situated in the heart of downtown and is connected to Montréal’s transit system and underground network:
  • Metro Connection: The hotel is directly connected to the Bonaventure Station (Orange Line) via the underground level.
  • Underground City (RESO): The venue is part of the extensive underground network, allowing access to shopping, dining, and other key venues (including the Palais des congrès convention centre).

Arriving by Bus

The conference venue is highly accessible by city bus. Several STM bus routes stop conveniently near the hotel, including major stops like René-Lévesque / Mansfield.
  • These routes connect the downtown core with the suburbs and serve the area extensively.
  • Recommendation: For real-time scheduling and precise route planning from your departure point, we highly recommend using a transit application such as the official STM-recommended Chrono or Transit app or Google Maps.

Arriving by Car

  • Parking: The Fairmont The Queen Elizabeth offers on-site valet parking and self-parking options (fees apply).
